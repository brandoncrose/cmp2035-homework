Continuous Publication

AMS Publications is pleased to announce that journal articles are now being published as each individual article completes production, shortening the time to publication even further than the recent historic lows. This continuous publication (CP) workflow is now in place for all ten of the AMS technical journals. Look for the "Issue in Progress" link on the AMS Journals Online site!
